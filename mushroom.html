Mushroom Gravy

Description

Mushroom Gravy is a simple yet delicious recipe which can be made under 20 minutes. It is an excellent companion for rice, Chapathi and roti.

Mushrooms are low in calories and rich in important nutrients like Selenium, Potassium , Vitamin D and lot more.

Ingredients:

  • Mushroom(250g)
  • Oil(3 tbsp)
  • Cinnamon stick(1)
  • Cardomom(3)
  • Cumin seeds (1/2 tsp)
  • Onion (1)
  • Turmeric (1/2 tsp)
  • Garam masla (1 tsp)
  • Coriander Powder (1 tsp)
  • Chilli powder (2 tsp)
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Green chillies
  • Curry leaves
  • Tomato
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Coriander leaves

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cinnamon stick, cardomom and cumin seeds, after a few seconds add finely chopped onions, salt and saute until golden.
  2. Once the onion are golden add the masalas - Giner garlic paste, turmeric powder, Cumin powder, garam masala, coriander powder, red chilli powder, green chillies and curry leaves. Mix all the ingredients well and then add chopped tomato, little bit of water and cook until everything turns soft (5-6 minutes). You can also add tomato paste to get very smooth gravy.
  3. Once oil starts to separate, add the cleaned and sliced mushrooms and ix it well with the masala mixture and cook 3 - 4 minutes. Then add required water and cook until the mushrooms are fully done.
  4. Finally, garnish with chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat. You can add a tsp of ghee on the top to enhance the flavor.
